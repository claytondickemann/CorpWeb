Services

24/7 Delivery of Petroleum Products

Let Avalon help your business to best meet your fueling needs. Avalon offers major brand gasoline and diesel from terminal sources with 24/7 flexible delivery via transport loads, tank wagon or directly into your vehicles or equipment. We work to reduce risk and add value for our customers with our industry-leading experience, exceptional service, vast supplier relationships and competitive pricing.

Nighttime Unit Fill

A great way to reduce expenses while your fleet or equipment is sitting idle after hours at night. Eliminate yard congestion and costly driver downtime. Avalon evaluates your schedule and yard layout, and efficiently fuels your vehicles or equipment at night so that they’re ready to go by the next morning. A delivery receipt is provided that lists the unit #’s, and individual and cumulative gallons dispensed.

Installation of Above Ground Storage Tanks

Gain on-site control over fuel inventories and the convenience of on-site fueling with above ground tank installation. Avalon provides pumps and dispensing equipment that are matched to your business’ fueling needs to ensure quick fueling, the ability to track gallons dispensed and zero down time risk. We offer flexibility with tank capacity and placement, and tank repair and parts replacement service.

Valvtect Maine Fuel Service

Differentiate your marine marketing fuel supply program from your competitors. Avalon’s marine fuel includes Valvtect Octane Performance Improver additive for gasoline and the Premium Diesel Program for diesel fuel. Valvtect’s marketing program entitles you to free pump toppers, point of purchase displays and brochures, signage, customer rebates and free advertising in boater magazines.

Complete Lubricant Lines & Additive Technology

Offers manufacturer direct additives, lubricants, oils and absorbents in quarts or bulk quantities. From Automotive-to-Marine-to-Heavy Duty Truck applications, EPA compliance and elimination of sub-zero weather risk. Same day or emergency services available.

Automated Fuel Station

Fill up your vehicle with competitively priced B11 biodiesel using Avalon’s automated key card system at our Markham, IL facility. Convenient hours are between, 5:30am and 5:30pm, Monday through Friday of each week (excluding holidays). Customers must be preapproved, and assigned an Avalon key card.
