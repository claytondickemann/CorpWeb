Programs

Keep Fill Program

No more worries about running out of fuel. Take the guesswork out of fuel deliveries, and minimize the number of deliveries to save dollars and deal with less paperwork.

Avalon’s Keep Fill program utilizes state-of- the-art SMARTank wireless technology to continuously monitor the level of fuel in your tanks, and ensures that you always have an appropriate fuel supply to meet your operational needs.

SMARTank monitors and tracks fuel inventory for both in ground and above ground tanks via a website, and you can also obtain access to monitor your fuel right along with us. Monitors gauge fuel levels, daily, and will also send out a special real time alert if a tank level drops to a predetermined threshold.

Transportation Logistics Software/Hardware

Avalon processes orders and dispatches them efficiently and by utilizing the latest technology. When Avalon makes a delivery, the fuel dispensed is digitally measured to provide the greatest degree of accuracy for our customers.

Avalon utilizes SMARTruck, a satellite-driven software/hardware technology, to process fuel orders,manage dispatch functions and digitally capture delivery information. When delivering fuel, our drivers utilize a GPS tracking device to scan bar codes that have been placed on each of our customer’s vehicles and pieces of equipment. Each scan will register the unit, and fuel is dispensed and measured with the utmost degree of accuracy and security. Our trucks are set up with a server and printer and, upon each delivery, an electronic receipt with detailed fuel delivery information is generated (i.e. date, type of fuel, # of gallons dispensed, etc.). This technology provides Avalon with an efficient fleet management tool which allows us to best serve our customers.

ULSD Future Fixed Pricing

Eliminate the risk and worry associated with volatile fuel prices.

Avalon’s ULSD future fixed rate pricing will provide you with a fixed fuel price or periodic prices for your future designated fuel needs. You can lock in at a future price or prices, and this provides a hedge against future fuel price risk. This pricing structure will protect you against unexpected and volatile future price swings in the market due to potential economic uncertainty or downturn, global events, etc.
